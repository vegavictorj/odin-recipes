Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a quick, easy and simple breakfast recipe you cannot go wrong with.

On top of having great nutrition values, it is very affordable.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 2 slices of Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 green onions (optional)
  • 2 slices of bread of your choice (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the slices of ham and the slices of Cheddar cheese into dices
  2. Chop green onions to small pieces (optional)
  3. Whisk eggs in a small bowl until smooth
  4. Mix the Cheddar cheese, the salt and the pepper with the eggs
  5. Preheat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
  6. Poor in the ham and cook until lightly colored
  7. Add the egg mixture
  8. Toast slices of bread
  9. Cook and stir until set but still moist (3-5mins)
  10. Serve and sprinkle chopped green onions
  11. Enjoy your delicious breakfast!
